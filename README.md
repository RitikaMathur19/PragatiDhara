1. Executive Summary
PragatiDhara is an AI-powered, crowd-sourced sustainable traffic optimization platform designed to reduce congestion, improve urban mobility, and minimize fuel wastage. 
The platform combines citizen participation with green AI models to recommend eco-friendly routes, dynamic rerouting, and ride-sharing incentives. 
Unlike traditional systems that rely solely on cameras and infrastructure, PragatiDhara leverages data directly from commuters — turning them into active contributors toward a sustainable future.
2. Vision & Mission
Vision: To make Indian cities cleaner, smarter, and more sustainable by transforming how citizens contribute to traffic management.
Mission: To create an intelligent mobility ecosystem where every commuter action contributes to reduced congestion, lower emissions, and collective sustainability goals.
3. Problem Statement & Current Pain Points
India faces severe traffic congestion, especially in Tier-1 cities, leading to daily economic and environmental losses. 
Key pain points include:
- Lack of citizen participation in traffic data sharing.
- Overreliance on cameras and static IoT sensors.
- Limited coordination between navigation apps and city traffic systems.
- High fuel wastage and CO₂ emissions due to inefficient routing.
- Poor integration of green mobility initiatives (EVs, ride-sharing, etc.).
